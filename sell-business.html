Sell-Side Advisory for Business Owners

Confidential, structured, and execution-focused support for business owners preparing for a sale, ownership transition, or planned exit.

Selling a business is one of the most significant financial decisions an owner can make. CertaServe Partners helps clients prepare for that process with disciplined financial review, organized documentation, and practical transaction support designed to improve readiness, reduce risk, and support a smoother path to closing.

What We Help You Achieve

  • A clearer understanding of business value drivers, risks, and readiness
  • More organized financial and operational documentation
  • Improved preparedness for buyer questions and diligence requests
  • Better control of confidential information throughout the process
  • Structured support from preparation through transaction execution

Our Approach

  • 1) Initial Assessment: clarify your objectives, timeline, priorities, and transaction context
  • 2) Financial & Business Review: review historical performance, business structure, key contracts, and major risk areas
  • 3) Readiness & Positioning: improve the quality of information and prepare materials for informed buyer discussions
  • 4) Buyer Information Support: help manage information flow, document requests, and responses in a controlled way
  • 5) Due Diligence Support: organize diligence materials and reduce friction during the review process
  • 6) Closing Support: assist with coordination and preparation through the final stages of the transaction

Typical Information Requested

  • Two to three years of financial statements and/or tax returns
  • Year-to-date financial information
  • Summary of customers, suppliers, and revenue concentration
  • Key contracts, lease details, and major commercial commitments
  • Staffing overview and operating structure
  • Inventory, equipment, and significant asset information
  • Known operational, financial, or compliance issues relevant to a transaction

Confidentiality Matters

Confidentiality is essential in any sale process. We support clients in managing the release of information carefully and appropriately, based on the stage of the transaction and the seriousness of the interested party.
