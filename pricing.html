Engagement & Fees

Clear, flexible engagement structures aligned with the scope, complexity, and objectives of each advisory assignment.

Our Approach

Transparent and tailored to your needs

Each engagement is structured based on the nature of the work, the level of analysis required, and the complexity of the decision or transaction involved.

We prioritize clarity, fairness, and alignment with client objectives when determining fees. Engagement terms are discussed upfront so expectations are clear from the outset.

Engagement Models

Flexible structures depending on the assignment

Project-Based Engagement

Fixed or scoped pricing for defined advisory work such as due diligence, valuation support, financial modeling, or transaction preparation.

Advisory Retainer

Ongoing support for business owners and investors requiring regular access to financial insight, strategic input, and decision support.

Transaction Support

Engagements structured around key transaction stages, including preparation, review, and execution support.

Custom Engagement

Tailored arrangements for complex or multi-phase assignments where flexibility and adaptability are required.

What Influences Fees

Factors considered in structuring engagements

  • Scope and complexity of the assignment
  • Depth of financial analysis required
  • Timeline and urgency
  • Level of involvement across transaction stages
  • Coordination with other advisors (legal, tax, financing)
What to Expect

A structured and transparent process

We aim to ensure that every engagement begins with a clear understanding of objectives, scope, and expected outcomes.

  • Initial discussion to understand your situation and objectives
  • Clear definition of scope and deliverables
  • Transparent fee structure agreed upfront
  • Regular communication throughout the engagement
