Due Diligence & Valuation

Independent financial analysis to help buyers, investors, and business owners assess risk, validate assumptions, and make better-informed decisions.

CertaServe Partners provides disciplined due diligence and valuation support for transactions, investments, and strategic reviews. We help clients go beyond surface-level information by identifying key financial issues, testing assumptions, and highlighting the factors that matter most to value and decision quality.

What We Support

  • Financial due diligence support
  • Earnings and cash flow analysis
  • Working capital and balance sheet review
  • Identification of financial risks and red flags
  • Valuation support and pricing considerations
  • Scenario analysis and decision support

When This Is Valuable

  • Before acquiring a business or investing capital
  • When testing whether historical performance supports the asking price
  • When assessing downside risks, hidden issues, or weak assumptions
  • When preparing for negotiation, financing, or internal approval

Our Approach

  • Review: understand the transaction, opportunity, and available financial information
  • Assess: analyze earnings quality, cash flow, balance sheet items, and key assumptions
  • Identify: highlight risks, inconsistencies, and areas requiring clarification
  • Support: provide structured insight to strengthen negotiation and decision-making

Why It Matters

A transaction can look attractive at first glance while still carrying meaningful financial and operational risk. Proper diligence and valuation support help reduce uncertainty, improve pricing judgment, and strengthen confidence before capital is committed.
